Welcome to optiMize.

optiMize is a student-driven social innovation movement at the University of Michigan.

We're a community of students and mentors working together year-round to turn ideas into impact.
Our Social Innovation Challenge supports student innovators with workshops and more than $100k in grants.
We don't have all the answers...far from it. But we've all asked ourselves, "why not me?" and taken action.

We hope you'll join us.
